About Me

I’m a former Front-End Developer with three years of experience creating clean, user-friendly designs and interactive web experiences. Over time, my curiosity evolved from how applications are built to how they operate securely and reliably in the cloud, which inspired my transition into Cloud Engineering.


Since then, I have earned my CompTIA Network+ and Security+ certifications and discovered a real passion for cloud technology. I’m now focused on building projects, deepening my technical skills, and pursuing a career that blends creativity, problem-solving, and security in the cloud space.

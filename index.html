About Me

I’m a former Front-End Developer with three years of experience creating clean, user-friendly designs and interactive web experiences. Since then, I have earned my CompTIA Network+ and Security+ certifications and discovered a real passion for cloud technology. I’m now focused on building projects, deepening my technical skills, and pursuing a career that blends creativity, problem solving, and security in the cloud space.
